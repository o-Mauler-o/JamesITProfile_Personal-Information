Personal Information

General Information

I’m James Stathakis, and welcome to my TED Talk personal profile. I am 20 years old, born in Sydney, moved to Canberra when I was young, and remained there until I was an adult. When COVID hit in 2020, I moved down to Tasmania to be with my parents, where I have remained up to present. I like sports, with my main preferred sport being AFL, but with an interest in Field Hockey. I played both when I was young, but stopped playing AFL upon entering Year 11. For around 14 years or so, I have been playing Video Games as my past-time and I have become something of a Gamer. It is only natural that I would go one step further and pursue a further education in IT. Friends and Family have always stated that I’ve had a knack for all things computers and IT, and I thought, why not actually make it official. In 2016/17, I started a bit into Web-Design as a hobby, and although I learnt a few things, it never really went anywhere.

Childhood

My childhood is pretty suburben and standard, growing up in Sydney and moving to Canberra when I was a kid, I left all my friends behind when I moved. However, I made friends when I moved to Canberra, and there I stayed until I was 19. Going through Primary and High School, I had a soft-spot for Mathematics, doing really well in it, but that interest dropped off upon heading into Tertiary Education. In 2007, by sheer chance, I was put onto Video Games, and that is when I changed, for better or worse.

Education & Employment

I started my primary education at Abbotsfield Public School in Sydney, in 2006. In April 2007, I moved to Canberra and started at Forrest Primary School. In 2012 I finished Grade 6 and the next year, I started my secondary education at Telopea Park High School. This was a bi-lingual school where I studied French for four years. In 2017, I graduated from Grade 10 and moved on to Grade 11, and my tertiary education at Narrabundah College. I studied a wide range of subjects in grades 11 and 12 including History (Both Modern and Ancient), Mathematics, English, Excersize Science, Physics, IT, CAD Industrial Designs and Architecture.

Like a lot of Teens, my first employment, I started in Hospitality, working in two sister cafes, where I learnt a few things, including cooking, cleaning and barista work. Although the work was horrendous, I loved making Coffees and continued my passion ever onward. My employment however came to a grinding halt when COVID-19 hit and I moved down to Tasmania to be with my parents during these trying times. However, the move cost me my employment, and there was little choice down in Tasmania.
